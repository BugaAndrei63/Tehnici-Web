F.A.Q

Why choose us?

We know there are other competitive platform other than us out there, but the thing we offer is a tight knit community.
Meaning great customer support with real people, 100% of the game profits to the rightful creators and an easy to use, awesome platform!

What is the cut the site takes from game sales?

0% ,we think the developers need to be rewarded for their awesome work, not us!

How do I get my game on the site?

Just create an account and you'll be presented with a step by step tutorial.

What if I want my game to be free?

No problem with that, we encourage first timers to have a free game or demo on our website so the people can get a fell for what's to come!
